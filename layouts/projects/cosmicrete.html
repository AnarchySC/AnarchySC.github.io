概要 Overview §01

Cosmicrete is cement that’s been whipped full of tiny, stable bubbles — producing a block that weighs less than half its conventional equivalent while outperforming R-6 foam board for insulation.

The secret ingredient is xanthan gum — a thickener from your local grocery store. It neutralizes cement’s aggressive water absorption long enough for dish soap to generate and stabilize bubbles directly inside the mix. No foam machine. No pre-generated foam. Just stir and watch it expand.

手順 Instructions §03
▶ PART 01 // GEL BASE — ゲルベース
01
Pre-wet the Xanthan
キサンタンガムの予備加水
Add 15g of xanthan gum to a large container. Add a small splash of rubbing alcohol (or mouthwash) and stir into a paste. This prevents clumping when water is added later.
02
Add Dish Soap
洗剤を追加
Stir in 60ml (¼ cup) of Dawn Ultra dish soap until combined with the xanthan slurry.
03
Add Water & Wait
水を加えて待機
Pour in 1 litre of water and stir. Leave to sit for 5–10 minutes until the mixture thickens into a gel. This is your Cosmicrete base.
Why it works: The hydrated xanthan slows cement’s water absorption, protecting the soap’s bubble-forming ability inside the hostile alkaline environment.
▶ PART 02 // MIX & POUR — 混合と注型
04
Suit Up
保護具装着
Put on your dust mask before opening the cement.
⚠ Dry cement dust is hazardous. Wear a mask throughout mixing.
05
Combine Gel + Cement
ゲルとセメントの混合
Combine 1 part gel base to 2 parts dry Portland cement by volume. It will look too dry at first — keep mixing. Bubbles form automatically as you work.
06
Stir to Target Density
目標密度まで撸拌
This is your only variable. Keep stirring and watch it expand. Draw marks on your container to hit the same density batch after batch.
Pro move: Mark your container at two points — your preferred density line, and the “bubble pop” limit for open-cell foam.
07
Pour Immediately
すぐに注型
Pour into your mold. Cosmicrete holds its shape like stiff meringue and is far easier to work with than regular concrete.

混合方法 Mixing Methods §04
🥄
By Hand
手動撸拌
Spatula or paint stick. Works fine for small batches. Most effort, full control.
→ Up to 75% air
⚙
Drill + Paddle
ドリル＋パドル
Best for 5-gallon bucket batches. Fast, consistent, and achieves maximum foam density.
→ Up to 75% air
🔄
Cement Mixer
セメントミキサー
Viable at scale with standard equipment. Mix gets too light to tumble properly at higher densities.
→ ~50% air
