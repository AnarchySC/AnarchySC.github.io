VMware vSphere / ESXi

Web consoles eat normal clipboard. Oblysk hotkeys bypass it entirely via keystroke simulation.

Cloud
AWS CloudShell & iDRAC

Browser-based terminals where Ctrl+V doesn't work. Oblysk types the text directly — no clipboard needed.

Enterprise
VDI & Remote Desktop

Corporate environments that lock clipboard redirection. Your 9 cells travel with you on the local machine.
